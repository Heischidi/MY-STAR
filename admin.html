Deploy to Vercel — make photos visible to everyone

📌 Why this step? Files uploaded in the admin are saved to this browser only. To make them visible to your girlfriend on Vercel, you need to put the actual files in your project and generate a media.json config.

1
Copy your image/video files into the images/ folder inside your STAR project. Name them anything you like (e.g. met.jpg, date.mp4).
2
Fill in the file paths below for each timeline slot and gallery item, then click Download media.json.
3
Replace the media.json in your STAR folder with the downloaded one.
4
Push to GitHub and deploy on Vercel — everyone will see the photos! 🎉
